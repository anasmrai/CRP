Change Response Profile

16 questions, 25 minutes. Every answer sounds reasonable — choose what genuinely reflects how you think and act, not what sounds best. Results are revealed with full explanations when you submit.

Sniff
Detects signals of change early. Pauses to read the pattern before acting.
Scurry
Moves fast on incomplete information. Values momentum over certainty.
Hem
Resists change — often through caution, approval-seeking, or delay framed as prudence.
Haw
Adapts deliberately. Once the change makes sense, moves forward and updates their approach.
